About Us

Our mission, values, and story

Members

  • RS Designs Team

Our Mission

  • Training Excellence: Deliver high-quality design instruction rooted in traditional techniques and philosophy.
  • Personal Development: Foster physical fitness, mental focus, and emotional resilience in every student.
  • Inclusivity: Provide opportunities for all, regardless of background or style, to participate and grow.
  • Community Engagement: Use design as a tool for empowerment, outreach, and positive social impact.

Our Vision

  • Empowerment & Growth: To empower individuals of all ages to build confidence, discipline, and resilience through design.
  • Community Impact: To create a supportive studio that uplifts the local community, especially youth, by promoting respect, unity, and positive values.
  • Tradition & Legacy: To preserve the rich traditions of design while adapting to modern needs, ensuring the art thrives for future generations.
